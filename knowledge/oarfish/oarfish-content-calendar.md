# Oar Fish Weekly Content Calendar

This is the standing weekly cadence. Every week follows it unless a drop launch overrides.

## Standing schedule

| Day | Platform | Format | Hook pattern | CTA pattern | Time |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | IG, FB | Hero image | New drop is live, weird fits only | Shop the drop now | 12:00 |
| Tue | TikTok, IG Reels | Carousel (educational) | "X reasons" or "Here's why" | Save and flex your weird knowledge | 18:00 |
| Wed | X (Twitter) | Meme | Wallet pain, drop FOMO, relatable misfit | Tag a friend who gets it | 13:00 |
| Thu | IG, FB | Behind the scenes | Sketchbook to streetwear | React if you rock with the process | 17:00 |
| Fri | IG, FB, TikTok | Carousel (story) | Surfaced from the abyss, lore drop | Swipe through and join the chapter | 19:00 |
| Sat | IG, FB | Testimonial / social proof | Real people. Real fits. Real weird. | Join the weirdos | 14:00 |
| Sun | IG, TikTok, X | Meme | Outfit when you ignore society | Be unapologetically weird | 11:00 |

## Format rules

**Monday hero.** One image. Caption opens with a fact (drop is live, vol number, creature name). Three to five lines max. Hashtags from the standard stack.

**Tuesday educational carousel.** Six to eight slides. Big claim on slide one. Evidence on slides two to seven. CTA on the final slide. Topics rotate: drop model, illustration process, fabric weight, cryptid lore, fit guide, sketchbook to print.

**Wednesday meme.** Borrowed format, Oar Fish punchline. Self-aware. Caption is the second beat, not a translation. Never explained.

**Thursday BTS.** Sketchbook footage, marker on paper, screen prints, pattern cutting, packing orders. Caption is process-honest. No motivational framing.

**Friday story carousel.** Lore drop tied to a creature in the current drop. Six to ten slides. Last slide links to the drop. Caption stays out of the way of the story.

**Saturday social proof.** Real customer photos, with permission. Caption mirrors what the customer said in their original post, does not paraphrase. Tag and credit.

**Sunday meme.** Lighter than Wednesday. Outfit-flex anti-normal energy. End on a brand line, not a CTA.

## Override: drop launch week

A new drop overrides the calendar for the launch week. Pattern:

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| -7 | Tease (cropped illustration only, no creature reveal) |
| -3 | Reveal (full hero image, creature named) |
| -1 | Countdown (lore drop carousel, drop time stated) |
| 0 | Launch (hero across all platforms, email, SMS) |
| +1 | First customer fits (if fast-shipping customers post) |
| +3 | Last call (when stock falls below 30%) |
| +7 | Sold out post or final units, closing the chapter |

## Hashtag stack

Always: #OarFish #WeirdIsThePoint #Streetwear

Drop-specific: #Vol01, #ForestGhost, #DesertWanderer, #Sunwalker, #FromTheDeep, #CryptidCore

Variable (pick two to three): #NoRestocks #LimitedDrop #HandDrawn #WearTheWeird #BeWeird #CustomerFits #OutfitCheck

Never use more than seven hashtags per post. Never use trending hashtags that don't fit the brand.
