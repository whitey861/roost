# Oar Fish Community Drop Program

One user-submitted drawing per month becomes a real Oar Fish drop. This file defines how the program runs.

## The pitch (audience-facing)

> Send us your weirdest creature. We pick one a month and turn it into real clothes. You get credit, a free hoodie of your design, and a cut of the drop. Drawing must be yours. Marker and paper preferred. Sketchbook scans accepted.

Hashtag: #SurfacedBy
Submission tag for IG: #OarFishCryptid

## Submission mechanics

**How to submit.** Two channels:

1. Email: submit@oarfish.store with image attached
2. Instagram: tag @oarfish and use #OarFishCryptid in the caption (we DM to confirm)

Email is the primary record. Instagram is the discovery layer.

**What we want.**

- Original drawing of a creature, monster, cryptid, or strange being
- Hand-drawn (marker, pen, pencil all fine)
- Photo or scan of the original, not a digital redraw
- A name for the creature
- One to three sentences on what it is or where it lives

**What we don't want.**

- AI-generated images (we will reverse-image-search)
- Tracings of existing characters or copyrighted creatures
- Anything from Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander culture, or other living Indigenous mythology
- Hate symbols, sexual content, or shock-gore
- Submissions for someone else's drawing

**Age rule.** Submitters must be 13 or older. Submitters under 18 need a parent or guardian to confirm consent by email before we can produce the design. We collect that consent before any printing happens.

## Selection

**Who picks.** Paul picks the shortlist of three. Community votes the winner via Instagram story poll over 48 hours.

**Selection criteria.**

- Most Oar Fish (kid-drawn, weird, specific)
- Has a story, not just an image
- Translates to print (works on a hoodie or tee at scale)
- Original beyond reasonable doubt

**Frequency.** One winner per calendar month. Announced first Friday of the month. Drops third Friday.

## What the winner gets

- Their drawing produced as a real drop (50 to 100 units depending on the line)
- Free hoodie or tee of their own design
- Credit on the product page (handle and creature name)
- $5 per unit sold, paid after the drop closes
- First-look at the next month's submissions

## What Oar Fish gets

- A community-driven drop every month
- A constant stream of content (BTS, voting, winner reveals, sold-out posts)
- A reason for new visitors to follow rather than just buy
- A growing library of cryptids that the audience helped surface

## IP and rights

**Submission terms** (to be displayed on the submission page and confirmed via the email reply or Shopify form):

> By submitting, you confirm:
>
> 1. The drawing is your original work
> 2. You're 13 or older (under 18 needs parent or guardian consent)
> 3. You grant Oar Fish a non-exclusive licence to reproduce your drawing on Oar Fish products and in marketing for that drop
> 4. You retain ownership of the original drawing
> 5. You can withdraw the submission any time before printing begins

**Royalty terms.** $5 per unit, paid 30 days after the drop closes. Paid by Stripe, PayPal, or bank transfer. Maximum payout per drop is $500 for a 100-unit run.

**Withdrawal.** Submitters can withdraw up to the day printing starts. If they win and withdraw before printing, we go to the runner-up.

## Marketing integration

The community drop generates content all month. Mapping to the standing calendar:

| Week | Day | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Tue | "Submit your cryptid" educational carousel (how to submit) |
| 1 | Fri | Three shortlist creatures revealed, vote in stories |
| 2 | Mon | Winner announcement (hero image, original drawing) |
| 2 | Thu | BTS: original drawing scanned, refined for print, mocked up |
| 3 | Mon | Drop hero (winner credited) |
| 3 | Fri | Lore carousel (winner's words on the creature) |
| 4 | Sat | Customer fits |
| 4 | Sun | Sold out post or last call meme |

This rhythm runs every month, on top of main-line and Growl content.

## Risks and mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Submitter claims their drawing was copied | Keep submission timestamps and original files. State winner in writing |
| Drawing turns out to be AI or traced | Reverse-image-search before announcing. Reserve right to revoke |
| Under-18 submitter, no parent consent | Hold production until consent received. Move to runner-up if not provided |
| Cultural sensitivity issue surfaces post-launch | Pull the drop, refund customers, public statement, learn |
| Low submission volume early | Seed with friends and existing Vol. 01 customers. Show the rhythm |
| Quality drops below brand standard | Paul holds veto. If no shortlist meets the bar, skip that month and say so |

## Skip-month rule

If no submission meets the brand standard, skip the month. Post the call again with examples of what we're looking for. Better to skip than print a weak drop. Audience respects honesty more than padding the calendar.
